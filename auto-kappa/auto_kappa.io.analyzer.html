auto_kappa.io.analyzer.get_kmode(volume, temp, frequencies, multiplicity, velocities, lifetime)

Calculate and return thermal conductivity of every modes

Parameters:

  • volume (float) – volume of a primitive unit cell [Bohr**3]

  • frequencies (ndarray, float, shape=(nk,nbands)) – eigenvalues [1/cm]

  • multiplicity (array, integer, shape=(nk)) – multiplicity

  • velocities (ndarray, float, shape=(nk,nbands,MULTI_MAX,3)) – group velocity [m/s]

  • lifetime (ndarray, float, shape=(nk,nbands)) – lifetime [ps]

  • nk (integer) – # of kpoints and bands

  • nbands (integer) – # of kpoints and bands

  • temp (float) – temperature [K]

  • mmax (integer) – maximum multiplicity

Returns:

  • kappa (ndarray, float, shape=(3,3)) – thermal conductivity

  • kmode (ndarray, float, shape=(nkpoints,nbands,3,3)) – thermal conductivity of each mode
